Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

We generally operate on a first-come, first-served basis, but we do accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Is your café wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our café is fully wheelchair accessible with ramp access and accessible restrooms.

Do you offer WiFi?

Yes, we provide complimentary high-speed WiFi for all our customers.

Can I host a private event at your café?

Absolutely! We offer private event bookings during and after regular hours. Please contact us for availability and pricing.
