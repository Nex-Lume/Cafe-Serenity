Our Story

Founded in 2016, Café Serenity was born from a passion for bringing people together over a perfect cup of chai and coffee. Inspired by India’s rich tea and coffee culture, we envisioned a warm and inviting space where tradition meets modern flavors.

What started as a cozy café in the heart of the city soon became a beloved gathering spot, known for its handcrafted beverages, soulful food, and serene ambiance. Despite our growth, we remain committed to authenticity, sustainability, and creating experiences that feel like home.

Every cup we serve is sourced from India’s finest coffee estates in Chikmagalur and Coorg, and our chai blends pay homage to time-honored recipes. Our food menu celebrates regional flavors, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to craft dishes that bring comfort and joy.
